Everyone's favorite elephant stars in this heartwarming and
timeless story for readers of all ages. In the colorful
Jungle of Nool, Horton discovers something that at first seems
impossible: a tiny speck of dust contains an entire miniature
world--Who-ville--complete with houses and grocery stores and even a
mayor! But when no one will stand up for the Whos of Who-ville, Horton
uses his elephant-sized heart to save the day. This tale of compassion
and determination proves that any person, big or small, matters,
can choose to speak out for what is right.
Horton is kind and trustworthy, but the lazy bird Mayzie
takes advantage of this good nature when she leaves Horton
to watch her unhatched egg. Everyone laughs at Horton as he sits on Mayzie's egg. Horton's kindness and faithfulness are sorely tested when he, and the egg, are kidnapped and sold to a circus - but his reward for being faithful is more wonderful than he could ever have dreamed!
