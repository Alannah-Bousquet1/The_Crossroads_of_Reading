This charming book introduces young children to words that rhyme,
such as Hop and Pop, Cup and Pup, Mouse and House, Tall and Small.
And once they have learned to recognize one word,
children soon find to their delight they can read another
simply by changing the first letter.
Illustrated in color. A fly went by, followed by a frog,
who's chased by a cat, who's chased by a dog and a host of
other frantic characters, in a humorous cumulative tale.
Pizza Pat stretches the floppy dough, puts on the gloppy tomatoes,adds the sloppy sausages, sprinkles on the cheese, and then pops it in the oven. But just before he cuts into his delicious pizza pie, he turns his back for one second--and the pizza disappears! A gang of hungry mice have taken Pat's irresistible creation away for themselves. Kids love pizza, and this cumulative book, based on the poem "The House That Jack Built", will whet their appetites for both pizza and books!
Are You My Mother? tells a very simple story for children who have just started to read. Their younger brothers or sisters will also want to follow the baby bird's quest as he asks everyone and everything he meets the question, "Are You My Mother?".
